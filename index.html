About Buddy

Buddy is a fun-loving little dog who is both chilled and hyper. He likes to walk in all of his favourite parks, and his favourite mode of transportation is on his daddy's motorbike.

Buddy has lots of doggy friends and human friends, but his best friend is a bird named Yoshi who is also his big brother. Buddy and Yoshi play together and have lots of fun. They are very close, and are always excited to see one another.

Buddy goes to work with his daddy sometimes, and is the most popular little guy in the entire building. He loves to receive cuddles, and play with his favourite toy with anyone who he can convince to play.

To know Buddy is to love him, so get to know him!
